# Weather Data Insights

## Temperature Trends
- Daily temperatures show seasonal fluctuations.
- Standard deviation indicates variability across months.

## Rainfall Patterns
- Monthly rainfall proxy (rainy days count) peaks during monsoon months.
- Dry months show minimal rainfall.

## Humidity vs Temperature
- Scatter plot reveals inverse relationship in certain seasons.

## Conclusion
Weather data highlights climate variability and supports sustainability awareness.
